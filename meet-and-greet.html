Meet and Greet Experience

The Classic Fan Experience

Get your chance to meet Kid Rock in person with our classic Meet and Greet experience. Available at select tour dates, this is your opportunity to briefly connect with Kid Rock before the show and take home memories that will last a lifetime.

What's Included:

  • Personal Meeting: Brief one-on-one time with Kid Rock
  • Professional Photography: Get a high-quality photo with Kid Rock
  • Autograph Opportunity: Have one item signed by Kid Rock
  • Early Access: Enter the venue before general admission
  • Exclusive Merchandise: Receive a special item only available to Meet and Greet participants
  • Premium Viewing: Special viewing area during the concert (where available)

Official Apple Partnership: Kid Rock has partnered with Apple to bring fans this exclusive opportunity.

Payment Information

The Meet and Greet experience requires a one-time payment of $1,000.

As part of our partnership with Apple Inc., payment must be made using an Apple Gift Card with the amount of $1,000.

To secure your spot:

  1. Purchase an Apple Gift Card worth $1,000 from any authorized retailer
  2. Complete the form below with your information
  3. Upload a photo of your Apple Gift Card in the form
  4. Submit the form to receive your confirmation and further details
