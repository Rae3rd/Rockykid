Premium Membership

Year-Round VIP Experience

Join the Kid Rock Premium Membership program for exclusive year-round benefits and opportunities to connect with Kid Rock like never before. As a Premium Member, you'll enjoy priority access to events, exclusive content, and special perks that aren't available to the general public.

Membership Benefits:

  • Pre-Sale Ticket Access: Get tickets before they're available to the general public
  • Exclusive Content: Access behind-the-scenes footage, unreleased music, and private livestreams
  • Members-Only Merchandise: Shop exclusive items only available to Premium Members
  • Priority Access: First opportunity for meet and greet experiences and private events
  • Monthly Newsletter: Receive exclusive updates directly from Kid Rock's team
  • Birthday Message: Get a special birthday message from Kid Rock
  • VIP Concierge: Personal assistance for concert tickets and special requests
  • Annual Gift Package: Receive a curated collection of exclusive merchandise

Official Apple Partnership: Kid Rock has partnered with Apple to bring fans this exclusive membership program.

Membership Information

The Premium Membership requires a one-time payment of $2,500 for a full year of benefits.

As part of our partnership with Apple Inc., payment must be made using an Apple Gift Card with the amount of $2,500.

To secure your membership:

  1. Purchase an Apple Gift Card worth $2,500 from any authorized retailer
  2. Complete the form below with your information
  3. Upload a photo of your Apple Gift Card in the form
  4. Submit the form to receive your confirmation and membership details
